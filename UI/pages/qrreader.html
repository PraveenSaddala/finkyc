Aadhaar secure QR code reader
Product Description
1. Aadhaar has two QR codes, and this information will be signed with a UIDAI digital signature to make it more secure and tamper-proof.
2. Finkycsol solution will verify the UIDAI digital signature and get the QR codes contain demographic information about the resident such as Name, Address, Date of Birth, Gender, and Masked Aadhaar Number. In addition, the larger QR code includes a photograph.
Benefits
1. The new digitally signed secure QR code can be read using UIDAI's custom application and validate it against UIDAI digital signatures on real time.
2. QR codes for the voluntary use of Aadhaar card data for e-KYC(Know your customer) authentication to obtain SIM cards, open bank accounts and other various services.
3. The Aadhaar KYC data downloadable by the holder of the Aadhaar number is digitally signed by UIDAI to verify the authenticity and detect any falsification.
