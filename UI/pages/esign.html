Aadhaar esign Services
Product Description
1. A digital signature uses principles of encryption to secure a document and ensure that authentication, integrity and non repudiation needs are met in much more secure and effective fashion than traditional, wet-ink signatures.
2. eSign authenticates the identity of an individual through Aadhaar eKYC information. An Aadhaar holder can sign a document upon his or her request and eSign facilitates issuing a Digital Signature Certificate.
Benefits
1. The offline mode of manually signing a document will continue to exist alongside eSign.
2. With eSign though, an individual can apply his or her signature from any location, at any time, involving any number of people and the digital signatures will be authentic and validated by Aadhaar.
3. This will enable businesses and service providers to have an edge over the competition if they do decide to deliver services through eSign.
4. Flexibility to accommodate varied systems, integration with customized API
